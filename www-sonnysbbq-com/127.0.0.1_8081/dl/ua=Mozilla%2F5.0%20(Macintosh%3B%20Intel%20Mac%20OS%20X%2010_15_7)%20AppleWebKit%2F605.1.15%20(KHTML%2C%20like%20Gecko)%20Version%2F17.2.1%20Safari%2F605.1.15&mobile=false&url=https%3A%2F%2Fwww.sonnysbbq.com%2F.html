Signature BBQ

Our meats are slow-smoked over oak for hours. In-house. Every day. That's what gives our BBQ its signature smokey flavor and tender, delicious taste.

Sauced & Tossed

Choose from St. Louis Ribs, Smoked Wings or Tenders and toss them in your favorite Sonny's BBQ Sauce.

BBQ Sandwiches

Make it a Big Deal: Add a Sidekick and a soft drink to any BBQ Sandwich for just a little bit more.

Premium Sidekicks

Amp up your meal with these premium sides.

Family Meals

Bring home the BBQ. Get somethin’ everyone can agree on and let us do the cookin’ with our family meals to go.

Pitmaster Picks

With so many slow-smoked favorites, how do you pick just one? You don't. Go with our Pitmaster Picks. Served with BBQ beans, coleslaw and garlic bread.

Appetizers

If you're eatin' BBQ, there's bound to be some sharing. Our appetizers are a good way to get the plate-passin underway.

BBQ By The Pound
